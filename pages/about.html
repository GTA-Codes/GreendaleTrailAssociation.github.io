The Greendale Trail Association is an organization that helps fund a new Greenbelt Trail System. The Greenbelt Trail System is developed in order to enhance pedestrian and cyclist safety across the Village, improve connectivity to existing pedestrian and bike infrastructure both within and outside the Village, attract cyclists to visit Downtown Greendale businesses, and provide expanded opportunities for diverse transportation options. The Trail would connect Greendale points of interest to the regional Oak Leaf Trail, a popular multi-use trail system across Milwaukee County.
